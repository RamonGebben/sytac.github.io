ReactJs dojo

A new way to learn technologies:
code, learn, have fun...

The Mission

We want to form a team to teach ourselves how to master new frontend technologies.

The Overall plan

We will start with a first epic which describes a working application with a minimal set of features. Such application will be developed for the web using ReactJs and the team is followed by one or more coach that guide the learning process.

How it works?

During the class a topic is briefly explained and a related assignment is made. The students work in pairs or small groups trying to solve the exercise.

What really happens?

Every developer has a different background, someone without any experience in frontend development. This melting pot forces us to face topics that sometimes seem simple as well and allows who already knows the subject to study it in the deep and discover something new.

Rules

  • We are here as peers: we all learn something, we can all teach something
  • We meet once a week after office hours
  • Anybody can join the group until we reach our maximum of 15 people
  • If you cannot commit to coming regularly to the group, you need to leave so that we can make room for someone else
